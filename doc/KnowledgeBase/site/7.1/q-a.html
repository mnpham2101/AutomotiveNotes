7.1 Tech Lead Tech Stack Overview

Q&A

  • Q: Why do automotive teams favor Jenkins over newer cloud-native CI (GitHub Actions, etc.)? A: On-prem licensing for tools like Trace32/Polyspace/HIL rigs, plus long-lived programs (10+ years of variant maintenance) favor self-hosted, customizable CI infra that integrates with specialized lab hardware — though this is gradually shifting as more tooling moves to containerized/cloud forms.
  • Q: What's the role of Autotools in a Yocto-based project if Yocto has its own recipe format? A: Many upstream open-source components Yocto packages (e.g. various GNU/Linux utilities) are themselves built with Autotools internally — a Yocto recipe for such a package often just wraps a standard ./configure && make && make install sequence via the autotools bbclass. Understanding Autotools helps when patching or debugging those upstream recipes.
  • Q: How would you justify investing in better static analysis tooling to a customer or management? A: Frame it in ASPICE/risk terms — static analysis catches MISRA/defect classes earlier (cheaper to fix at SWE.3/SWE.4 than after SYS.4 integration, see 6.1), directly reducing field-defect risk a customer cares about contractually.
